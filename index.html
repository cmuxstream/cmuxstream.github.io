xStream

Outlier Detection in Feature-Evolving Data Streams

xStream detects outliers in feature-evolving data streams, where new samples arrive as a stream, and the feature-space is unknown and evolves over time.

xStream is accurate in all three settings: (i) static data, (ii) row-streams, and (iii) feature-evolving streams, as demonstrated over multiple datasets in each setting.

xStream scales to over 1,000 point updates per-second (as witnessed on a commodity Xeon® machine running at 2.1GHz) while consuming bounded space that is user-configurable.

Emaad Manzoor, Hemank Lamba, Leman Akoglu. Outlier Dete'x'ion in Feature-Evolving Data Streams. In 24th ACM SIGKDD International Conference on Knowledge Discovery and Data mining (KDD). 2018.
