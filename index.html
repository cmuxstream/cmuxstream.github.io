xStream

Outlier Dete'x'ion in Feature-Evolving Data Streams

xStream detects outliers in streaming settings, where samples and feature are both streaming.

xStream is accurate as exhibhited on 3 settings: (a) Static (Known d, Known n), (b) Row-Streaming (Known d, Unknown n) and (c) Evolving Feature (Unknown d, Unknown n)

xStream is efficient with lesser run-time and memory requirements than other counterparts.

Emaad Manzoor, Hemank Lamba, Leman Akoglu. Outlier Dete'x'ion in Feature-Evolving Data Streams. In KDD. 2018.
